We Want to Hear From You

The Delta thrives on community engagement and reader input. Whether you have a story tip, feedback on our coverage, or questions about our reporting, we're here to listen.

What We're Looking For

We're particularly interested in stories that explore:

  • Technology Impact: How new technologies are changing industries, workplaces, and society
  • Startup Reality: Behind-the-scenes stories from Silicon Valley and beyond
  • Economic Shifts: How tech companies and innovations affect markets and employment
  • Innovation Access: Who benefits from technological progress and who gets left behind
  • AI Developments: Breakthroughs, controversies, and implications of artificial intelligence
  • Corporate Culture: The real stories behind tech company cultures and practices

Story Tips

Have insider knowledge about a tech company, startup, or industry development? We protect our sources and can arrange for confidential communication if needed. Include as much detail as possible about:

  • What's happening and why it matters
  • Key people involved
  • Timeline of events
  • Why this story needs to be told
  • Your connection to the story (if comfortable sharing)

Corrections and Feedback

We take accuracy seriously. If you spot an error in our reporting, please let us know immediately. Include:

  • The specific article and section
  • What information is incorrect
  • What the correct information should be
  • Any supporting documentation you have

Media Inquiries

For press inquiries, interview requests, or media partnerships, please use the same contact email with "MEDIA INQUIRY" in the subject line.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent story tips or corrections, please mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line.

Confidentiality

We understand that many of our sources need to remain confidential. We have established protocols for secure communication and source protection. If you need to discuss sensitive information, please indicate this in your initial contact.
