Editorial Policy

Our standards, ethics, and commitment to independent journalism

Editorial Independence

The Delta operates with complete editorial independence. Our reporting decisions are made by our editorial team based on journalistic merit, not commercial interests or external pressure. We do not accept editorial direction from advertisers, sponsors, or any external parties.

Accuracy and Verification

We are committed to accuracy in all our reporting. Every story undergoes rigorous fact-checking and verification before publication. When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently. Our sources are verified and, when possible, corroborated through multiple channels.

Source Protection

We protect our sources' confidentiality when requested. We do not reveal sources' identities unless we have explicit permission to do so. This commitment extends to protecting sources from retaliation, legal action, or other forms of harm.

Conflict of Interest

Our writers and editors must disclose any potential conflicts of interest, including financial relationships, personal connections, or other factors that might influence their reporting. When such conflicts exist, we either recuse the individual from coverage or clearly disclose the relationship to readers.

Corrections Policy

When we make factual errors, we correct them immediately and prominently. Corrections are clearly marked and explain what was wrong and how it has been fixed. We do not silently edit stories to fix errors without acknowledgment.

Opinion vs. News

We clearly distinguish between news reporting and opinion pieces. News stories are based on verifiable facts and multiple sources. Opinion pieces are clearly labeled as such and represent the views of individual writers, not necessarily The Delta as an organization.

Diversity and Inclusion

We strive to include diverse voices and perspectives in our coverage. This includes seeking sources from different backgrounds, industries, and viewpoints. We are committed to covering stories that affect underrepresented communities and ensuring our coverage is inclusive and representative.

Reader Engagement

We welcome feedback, corrections, and story suggestions from our readers. You can reach our editorial team at me@heg.wtf. We read all correspondence and respond to substantive feedback.

Ethical Guidelines

Our editorial team adheres to the highest standards of journalistic ethics, including:

  • Seeking truth and reporting it accurately
  • Minimizing harm to sources and subjects
  • Acting independently and avoiding conflicts of interest
  • Being accountable and transparent about our processes
  • Respecting privacy while serving the public interest

Updates to This Policy

This editorial policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or industry standards. Any significant changes will be communicated to our readers and posted prominently on our website.
